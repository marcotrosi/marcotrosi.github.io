phonetics

alternative-rock - This is my first band. My best friends Alexander Cee and Stefan Wehner founded a band in 1994, which I joined one year later. Singers, bass players and the band name changed a couple of times until we met Till Krause and Michael Kerth in 1998. We wrote and recorded songs and played gigs all over Germany. Definitely the best time in my life. Unfortunately our singer Till left the band in 2003. I thought I could replace him as a singer, but this never really worked.
