phonetics

alternative-rock This is my first band. My best friends Alexander Cee and Stefan Wehner founded a band in 1994, which I joined one year later. Singers, bass players and the band name changed a couple of times until we met Till Krause and Michael Kerth in 1998. We wrote and recorded songs and played gigs all over Germany. Definitely the best time of my life.
